无犯罪办理关键点
Police Clearance Key Points
  • 确认签发机关与适用范围（州/联邦、城市/省份）。
  • Confirm issuing authority and scope (state/federal, city/province).
  • 是否需要指纹卡、面签或线上授权。
  • Check if fingerprints, in‑person visit or e‑authorization is required.
  • 目的方要求：海牙还是使馆链路？是否要翻译？
  • Destination requirements: Apostille or consular? Translation?
学历认证关键点
Degree Verification Points
  • 准备学位证、成绩单，必要时联系院校验证。
  • Have diploma & transcripts ready; school verification may be needed.
  • 明确用途：工作签、人才项目、移民或继续教育。
  • Clarify purpose: work visa, talent program, immigration, further study.
  • 与后续签证/入职衔接时间匹配，避免空窗。
  • Align with visa/onboarding timelines to avoid gaps.
