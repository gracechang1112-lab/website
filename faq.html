FAQ

Q：海牙认证与领事认证有什么区别？
Q: What’s the difference between Apostille and Consular Legalization?
海牙认证适用于《海牙公约》成员国之间；非成员国通常需要“公证—外交部—使馆”的领事认证链路。
Apostille works among Hague Convention members; non‑members typically require the notary‑MOFA‑consulate chain.
Q：需要提供原件还是公证件？
Q: Do I need originals or notarized copies?
以目的方要求为准；部分国家接受经公证的复印件，可减少原件外寄风险。
Follow destination rules; some countries accept notarized copies to avoid shipping originals.
Q：无犯罪记录证明如何办理？
Q: How to get a police clearance certificate (PCC)?
国内可通过公安或政务平台开具，海外则依据签发地流程，可能要指纹卡或在线授权。
In China via PSB/government portals; overseas depends on jurisdiction and may need fingerprint cards or e‑authorization.
Q：教育部学历认证多久出结果？
Q: How long does MOE degree verification take?
一般 5–12 个工作日，可加急；以学籍核验响应速度为准。
Usually 5–12 business days, subject to school response; expedite options may apply.
Q：翻译是否必须？
Q: Is translation required?
目的国经常要求目的语或英文译本；我们支持多语种翻译并出具翻译声明。
Destination often needs translations; we provide multi‑language translation with declaration.
Q：可以同时走多条流程吗？
Q: Can different processes run in parallel?
可以，常见做法是认证与签证准备并行，以缩短总周期。
Yes, legalization and visa prep often run in parallel to shorten total turnaround.
Q：是否支持加急？
Q: Do you support expediting?
多数事项可加急，但受制于签发机关处理速度和预约量；我们会给出现实可行的时间窗。
Most items can be expedited but depend on authority workload; we’ll propose realistic time windows.
Q：费用如何构成？
Q: What’s included in the price?
报价通常包含官方费、服务费、快递/翻译等；超出范围会先沟通确认。
Quotes usually include government fees, service fees, and courier/translation; extras are confirmed beforehand.
Q：隐私与数据如何保护？
Q: How do you protect privacy and data?
最小化收集与分级存储，只用于办理和回访；完成后可按需删除。
Minimal collection and tiered storage; used only for service delivery and follow‑up; deletion upon request.

We arrange inbound/outbound courier with packaging and bundling guidance.
