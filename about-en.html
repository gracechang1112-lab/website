About Us

Global EDU Offer Limited serves foreigners in China and Chinese clients worldwide. We turn complex paperwork into clear step‑by‑step actions so you always know what to do next.

What We Do

  • Legalization: Apostille, consular legalization, HK China‑Appointed Attesting Officer (CAAO).
  • Police Clearance: Global PCC and China PCC, fingerprint card & remote authorization supported.
  • Education: MOE degree verification, study‑in‑China and study‑abroad planning & application.
  • Value‑adds: Translation, notarization, international courier, templates & checklists.

Why Choose Us

  • Compliance‑first: Strictly aligned to issuing and destination requirements.
  • Transparent ETA: Standard timelines with expedite options; milestone tracking.
  • 1:1 Consultant: Document review, checklists and responsive Q&A.
  • Global Network: Coverage across US/CA/UK/EU/AUS/NZ/SEA and more.
  • Data Security: Minimal collection, tiered storage, used solely for service delivery.
  • Clear Pricing: Single quote per item with government/service/courier fees broken out.

Typical Workflow

  1. Assessment: Purpose, jurisdiction and document status; confirm route & ETA.
  2. Prep: Checklists & templates; guide fingerprints/notary/translation if needed.
  3. Submission: Local/overseas filing in parallel; courier arranged when needed.
  4. Follow‑up: Milestones notified; rework (if any) handled quickly.
  5. Delivery: Scans first; originals shipped by tracked international courier.
  6. Aftercare: Usage guidance and advice on follow‑on applications.
