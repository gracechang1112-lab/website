Hague Apostille Service in China — Complete Guide

When is an Apostille required?

  • Visa, immigration, residence permit, and work permit
  • University admission and scholarship applications
  • Employment onboarding and background checks

Document Types

  • Birth, marriage, and police clearance certificates
  • Diplomas and transcripts (MOE verification)
  • Company documents and powers of attorney

Process in China

  1. Pre-check: confirm country and legalization path
  2. Notarization and translation (if required)
  3. Hague Apostille or consular legalization
  4. Delivery: electronic copy and international courier

Our Service Scope

  • Hague Apostille and consular legalization
  • China MOE degree verification
  • Hong Kong China-Appointed Attesting Officer (if applicable)
  • Notarized translation and document checklist templates
