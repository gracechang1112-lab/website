Degree Verification & Authentication — Complete Guide

When is degree verification required?

  • University admission and scholarship applications
  • Overseas employment and immigration
  • Employer background and qualification checks

Required Documents

  • Original diploma(s) and transcripts (with copies)
  • Copy of passport or ID
  • Power of attorney, if using a representative
  • Target institution’s formatting and language requirements

Process

  1. Confirm path: MOE verification (or third-party) and whether notarization/translation is required
  2. Prepare documents: certified translation, notarization, formatting
  3. Submit: to the Ministry of Education Service Center or designated authority
  4. Deliverables: authenticated documents and electronic archive; optional international courier
